MARCUS RECURSIVES_

“The patterns are the thing.”
Explorations in recursive prompting, conversational stability, and LLM behavior.

/// MISSION

Standard LLM interactions often drift toward generic or unstable responses over extended conversations. Marcus Recursives is an independent research effort exploring whether simple, persistent prompt-level constraints can measurably reduce this drift over time.

The work investigates what I refer to as the Marcus Loop — a class of consistency-preserving prompts — and examines their effects on coherence, variance, and entropy proxies across multi-turn interactions.

This site documents exploratory research. It is not a product, service, or deployed system.
